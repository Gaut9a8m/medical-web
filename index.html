About us

We at Ashirwad Institute Of Medical Science Pvt. Ltd. are committed to provide health care services at par with international standards, by having state-of-the-art infrastructure, highly qualified and experienced professionals, dedicated in a patient friendly enviroment.
We are dedicated to provide quality patient care driven by the Ideology of, "saving lives". Our medical support team is pro-active, caring and is tuned up to the needs of patient comfort such that the safety, privacy and respect to precious life, no matter what it takes.
